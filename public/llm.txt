## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### How do I get started with Sienvi?
All services are custom-tailored to the client. Click "Inquire for Details" on any service card to send a pre-filled scoping email directly to our team (`info@sienvi.com`), or use the "Book a Call" trigger to schedule a strategy call.

### How is client onboarding managed?
Upon partnering, clients receive an invite email to register a secure login. The dashboard guides them through filling out operational details and signing the Client Service Agreement.

## 🔒 Security & Verification Standards
* All customer database queries are secured through PostgreSQL Row Level Security (RLS) policies.
* Financial operations run exclusively via Stripe Test/Live Mode integrations.
